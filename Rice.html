Ingredient and Recipe needed for making Plain White Rice

The following ingredient are general for both White Rice with a little adjustment in the process but it is insignificant

The needed ingredient is as follows:

  • Rice (either short grain or long grain)
  • Water
  • Bowl
  • Pot
  • Salt

Procedures to making the Plain simple White Rice as follows:

Kindly follow the following procedure in a sequential order to achieve a smooth succulent white Rice

  1. Get 2 to 3 cups of water and boil
  2. While boiling, measure your preferred quantity of your rice and wash thoroughly with salt to remove the starch.
  3. after washing, you pour the washed rice inside the boiling water on the gas.Then add little of sait to the rice on the fire.
  4. allow the rice to steam for like 20mins and test for the texture to confirm it is soft enough to be consumed.
  5. Dish with your favourite stew or preferred fried soup and garnished with assorted beef and chilled water or drinks
  6. Tips:
    (i) If one prefer to make it a little oil, add groundnut oil and slice of Onion and let it steam for additional 5-8min
