Ingredient and Recipe needed for making Eba

The following ingredient are general for both White Garri or Yellow Garri, a little adjustment in the process but it is insignificant

The needed ingredient is as follows:

  • Garri (either whilte or Yellow)
  • Water
  • Spatula (Omo orogun, in Yoruba)
  • Bowl

Procedures to making the Eba as follows:

Follow the following procedure in sequential order to achieve a smooth Eba

  1. Get a quantity of water and boil
  2. When boil, share the water in ratio 80:20, to cater for the need of water after
  3. collect the 80% of the water and then apply the Garri
  4. While applying the garri, that should not be in a rush or much, rather in smooth flowing small quantity, till it fully circulate the water and with a very little water on the surface to make room for turning
  5. Evenly turn the garri till it give a little stiffness to avail room for testing the tenderness of the stature
  6. Once it hit the taste of our texture, we are done in the making
  7. Also, in the case their is lump (koko) in the eba, this is the part where the 20% reserve water come to play to achieve the desire smoothness
  8. Once this is to our personal taste, then we can serve with our favourite vegetable and Egusi (optional) to garnish it with assorted beef and Protein
