Ingredient and Recipe needed for a delicious and sumptuous Noodles

For our very sumtuous noodles we are going to use the following ingredients

The needed ingredient are as follows:

  • Noodle of our favourite brand, noodle and not spaghetti or Macaroni
  • Groundnut oil, in addition with the tiny one that comes with the Noodle
  • Bowl of water
  • Pepper (rodo, for a spicy chili) and/or mash with tomatoes to get fish stew
  • Seasoning, preferred the ones that comes with the Noodles
  • Less I forget onions

Lets get to prepare our Delicious Noodles, Shall we?

Kindly follow this step religiously in order to achieve a Harvard graduate sumptuous Noodles

  1. Get a very small unit of water and boil, a tea sauce cup for 2-3 small noodles and set it on fire for boiling
  2. While boiling, open up the noodles and collect the condiment that follows the noodles separately
  3. Then add a small unit of groundnut oil to blend with the boiling water, then introduce the sliced onions, let both steam.
  4. Allow to boil for like 3min, then inroduce the noodles into the boil water and reduce the heat and cover for additional 2-3min, by this time, the noodle can be turned to ensure the steam circulate as expected.
  5. By this time the sliced or diced pepper(rodo) and/or tomatoes should be introduced into the meeting, and turned to evenly steam and shared its aroma with the team
  6. Boil for additional 2-3min, keep checking to avoid burnt
  7. Tips:
    Optional: One can choose to add crayfish into the mix in the last run of 2-3min for it to add it own additional aroma. And there you have it. Our Noodles that go to Harvard is ready
  8. Dish with a chilled wine (either Alcoholic or non-alcoholic wine) or possibly a iced filled Chapman
